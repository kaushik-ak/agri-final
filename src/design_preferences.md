# Design System: AgriKisan (Prithvi-Tech)
 
## Theme & Style
**Concept**: "Digital Soil." A fusion of traditional Indian agricultural roots and future-forward AI technology.
**Mood**: Trustworthy, Empowering, Simple, and Vibrant.
**Mode**: Light Mode (Primary) for high visibility in outdoor/sunlight conditions; High-Contrast accessibility toggles.
 
## Color Palette
- **Primary (Growth Green)**: HSL 142 72% 29% - Used for headers, primary actions, and success states.
- **Secondary (Earth Umber)**: HSL 25 30% 20% - Used for deep contrast text and grounding elements.
- **Accent (Harvest Gold)**: HSL 45 93% 47% - Used for urgent alerts, "Apply Now" buttons, and AI highlights.
- **Background (Cloud White)**: HSL 0 0% 98% - Clean, breathable canvas.
- **Surface (Glass)**: White with 80% opacity and 12px blur - Used for cards to create depth.
 
## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Outfit' (Sans Serif) - Bold, modern, and highly legible in regional scripts.
- **Body**: 'Inter' - Optimized for readability on small mobile screens.
- **Hierarchy**:
  - H1: 40px / 1.2 line-height (Mobile: 32px)
  - H2: 32px / 1.3 line-height
  - Body: 18px / 1.6 line-height (Slightly larger than standard for better readability).
 
## Layout & Spacing
- **Grid**: 12-column Desktop, 4-column Mobile.
- **Spacing**: 8px base unit. Section padding: 80px (Desktop), 40px (Mobile).
- **Corner Radius**: 24px for main cards, 12px for buttons (creates a friendly, non-aggressive feel).
 
## Components
- **Buttons**: Large (min-height 56px), bold shadows (4px offset), haptic-feedback style hover states.
- **Cards**: Soft "Floating" effect with `0 10px 30px -10px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`.
- **Inputs**: High-contrast borders (2px) to ensure visibility on low-end mobile displays.
 
## Imagery & Icons
- **Imagery**: High-definition photography of Indian landscapes and diverse crops, treated with a subtle warm color overlay.
- **Icons**: Duo-tone "Flat-Clay" style icons—large, colorful, and instantly recognizable (e.g., a bright sun for weather).
 
## Motion & Animation
- **Philosophy**: Functional motion. 
- **Transitions**: 300ms ease-out for hover states.
- **AI Pulse**: A soft, glowing green ring around the voice search button to signify "Listening" or "Ready."
